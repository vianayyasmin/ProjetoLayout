Welcome to Sanrio!

Sanrio Company Ltd. is a Japanese company that is best known for creating and licensing popular characters and brands, including Hello Kitty. The company was founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji and is headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.

About

The Sanrio Company, Ltd. is a Japanese company that designs, licenses and produces products focusing on the kawaii segment of Japanese popular culture.
